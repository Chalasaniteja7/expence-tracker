addExpense Function: Adds a new expense to the list and saves it to localStorage. Updates the total amount and clears the input fields.
addExpenseToList Function: Adds an expense to the HTML list.
loadExpenses Function: Loads existing expenses from localStorage when the page is loaded.
clearAllExpenses Function: Clears all expenses from the list and localStorage.
Event Listeners: Handles button clicks and Enter key presses to add expenses or clear all.
